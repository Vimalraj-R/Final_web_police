About This Project

The AI-Powered Smart Parking System is an innovative initiative by the Tamil Nadu Police Department to revolutionize parking management in high-traffic urban areas. By leveraging advanced Machine Learning (ML) technologies, we aim to reduce traffic congestion, improve parking efficiency, and enhance overall urban mobility.

🔹 How It Works

  • 🚗 Users enter their destination
  • 🅿️ The system predicts nearby parking availability in real-time
  • 🚍 If the parking is full, alternative parking spots are suggested
  • 🛣️ Comprehensive navigation options provided (bus, auto, walking routes)

🔹 Key Features

  • ✅ Advanced ML-powered real-time parking prediction
  • ✅ Secure CCTV Monitoring (exclusively for police use)
  • ✅ Official Tamil Nadu Government Police Branding
  • ✅ Bilingual support (Tamil & English)
  • ✅ Fully responsive mobile interface
